Our Story

About Tkicks

Authentic Thrift. Iconic Kicks.

Founded in 2020, Tkicks began with a simple mission: make authentic sneakers accessible to everyone while promoting sustainable fashion. What started as a small collection of thrifted finds has grown into a trusted marketplace for pre-owned heat.

Every pair in our inventory undergoes a rigorous 10-point authentication process. We believe that great style shouldn't cost the earthliterally. By giving sneakers a second life, we're reducing waste and making iconic designs available at prices that make sense.
